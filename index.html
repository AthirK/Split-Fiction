About the Game

Split Fiction is a cooperative action-adventure game where two players control Mio and Zoe, writers trapped inside a chaotic simulation blending their sci-fi and fantasy stories. As they navigate surreal landscapes, they must work together to solve puzzles, defeat enemies, and reshape the narrative to escape. The game emphasizes teamwork, with unique character abilities and dynamic environments that shift based on player choices.

Main & side storys

Split Fiction's main story follows Mio and Zoe, rival writers trapped in a simulation blending their sci-fi and fantasy worlds, forcing them to cooperate to escape. Side stories are optional missions representing their unfinished tales, offering unique gameplay like sandfish riding or mountain climbing. These expand the lore and reveal more about their pasts.

mini games

Split Fiction features mini-games like Slopes of War, a snowboarding challenge with obstacles, and Gameshow, a tense bomb-passing platformer. Mountain Hike lets players climb with help from giants. Each mini-game adds unique mechanics and fun cooperative moments.

split screen

Split Fiction features seamless split-screen co-op, allowing players to team up locally or online. The screen dynamically adjusts based on player actions, merging or separating as needed. This enhances teamwork and keeps the gameplay immersive.

Jump into mind-blowing moments as you experience the many worlds of Split Fiction, a boundary-pushing co-op adventure from the studio behind It Takes Two. Play together with couch co-op or remotely with Friend's Pass.
